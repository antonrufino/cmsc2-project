Who We Are

Redefining fashion... Sice 1908

Wall is the realization of the dream of three fashion geniuses - Anton Rufino, Christie, Lemuel Maranan. Wall started out with humble beginnings. With money from their previous ventures, they started Chair Apparel, the precursor to Wall. Chair started out poorly with barely any sales. It is then that the three founders decided to go against the norm and redefine fashion forever. This resulted in a series of successive hit innovative clothing lines that would catapult Chair Apparel to the world stage.

Not a few years later, the company had to undergo a massive branding change when a lawsuit from Bench a certain clothing company, forced Chair Apparel to change its name to Wall. Now a leading brand in the world of high fashion, Wall continues to redefine fashion.
