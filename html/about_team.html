Meet the Team

Anton Rufino

CEO and Co-founder

Anton is among the chief visionaries who redefined not only Philippine fashion but also international fashion. His choice of career belies the fact that he graduated summa cum laude with a bachelor's degree in computer science from the University of the Philippines Los Banos (UPLB). Besides leading the company, he is also pursuing graduate studies at the Massachusetts Institue of Technology (MIT).

Christie

Cheif Fashion Designer and Co-founder

Christie is the leader the company's world renowned team of fashion designers. She was originally an undergraduate at UPLB studying computer science where she met Anton Rufino and Lemuel Maranan, both of whome would become the co-founders of Wall. She later transferred to the Julliard School in New York City to pursue studies in performing arts. She would reunite with Anton and Lemuel to form Wall.

Lemuel Maranan

Secretary of the Department of Redefinition and Co-founder

As the company's quirky and self-proclaimed Secretary of the Department of Redefinition (more commonly known as the Marketing Division), Lemuel is the chief force in creating the company's brand. It is also thanks to him that the company has a very lively and informal atmosphere which largely contributes to its massive creative output. He graduated summa cum laude from UPLB with a bachelor's degree in computer science. Currently, he is hailed as the second coming of Steve Jobs for his marketing prowess.
